“HOW TO” CHALLENGE STATEMENTS

Holistic (or Logical) Levels are a tool which allows us to analyze situations in our life through different perspectives. These perspectives are usually treated as a hierarchy:
1.) Purpose
2.) Identity
3.) Belief
4.) Values
5.) Skills & Capabilities
6.) Behavior
7.) Environment & Culture
The basic idea behind the Holistic Levels is that each level directly affects those lower in the hierarchy. All of us think through and communicate to ourselves and with others through these levels. As one moves through each level, thoughts become less trivial and more complex or abstract.
The key point is how the human mind structures thoughts and how one can take notice and consider the levels involved, both in problem solving and in the underlying logic itself. The Holistic Levels indicate the different degrees to which an individual thinks, feels, knows or speaks.
