Nivritti Creative Problem Solving (CPS©) is a set of framework, tools and techniques using a multi-dimensional approach arranged into various stages to address business as well as leadership/ individual challenges.

There are six interconnected stages in the CPS framework. This tool facilitates the first two stages of the framework, which are designed to help you identify, explore and articulate your specific issue.

The tool uses the word “problem” as a neutral term. Words like problem, issue, situation, goal, objective, opportunity could also be used, since each issue under consideration is unique.
