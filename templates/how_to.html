“HOW TO” CHALLENGE STATEMENTS

{# new change here #}

Additional Information and Examples:


Going back to our earlier examples, below are some potential “How to” challenge statements (in both “Moving Towards” and “Moving Away From” wording) for each one.
1.) Our organization needs to reduce the high rate of employee attrition.
  A. Moving Towards (pleasure or greater positive results)
   a) How to increase the percentage of employee retention?
   b) How to attract and retain top talent in the industry?
  B. Moving Away From (pain or negative results)
   a) How to reduce the high rate of employee attrition?
   b) How to decrease our hiring and training expenses?
2.) Our organization needs to reduce the high rate of employee attrition.
  A. Moving Towards (pleasure or greater positive results)
   a) How to get repeat orders and new customers?
   b) How to become a global leader in our industry sector?
  B. Moving Away From (pain or negative results)
   a) How to avoid losing customers and repeat orders?
   b) How to prevent a cash flow crisis this year?
